Markham
I was born in Toronto, Canada. A place I know little of, for I quickly moved
to the town of Markham. I grew up in an Asian community, near a Chinese mall called
Market Village. Living in the suburbs, I attented Aldergrove Public School until the eigth grade.
From there I went to Milliken Mills Highschool.

Although Markham is classified as a town, it has a large population, and is the largest
town in Canada. Markham is also called "Canada's Hi-Tech Capital" due to the presence of
large companies such as AMEX, Apple, ATI, IBM, Lucent, Motorola, Sun Microsystems and Toshiba.
