Market Village

Market Village is a Chinese mall located in Markham, Ontario, Canada. at the corner of
Steeles Avenue and Kennedy Road. Built in 1990 by Cedarland Properties Limited,
it attracted a large Chinese community. In 1997 "Pacific Mall" was build beside Market Village,
further attracting more people to this day.
Milliken Mills Highschool

Milliken Mills Highschool is part of the York Region District School.
Located on 7522 Kennedy Road near the Milliken Community Center and founded in 1988,
It is home to 1600 students, and has been known to produce students with both high
academic and athletic standards.
AMD Graphics Product Group

Formerly known as ATI Technologies, headquartered in Markham, it has recently been
acquired by AMD and renamed. ATI is known for producing GPUs(Graphics Processing Units)
for various electronics, and competes against Nvidia in the market.
